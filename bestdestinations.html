New-York

New York, the city that never sleeps! This city in the United States bustles day and night and there is something to do on every street corner. The dizzying skyscrapers, Central Park and the number of examples of highlights in The Big Apple. During a city trip in New York, get on your bike and discover the Harlem district full of soul or plan a shopping session. You will find plenty of shops here!

Los Angeles

When you think of a holiday in Los Angeles, you probably immediately think of the American film industry with the famous city of Hollywood ofcourse. In addition to a attractions such as the Walk of Fame or the Hollywood Sign, this major California city is home to a variety of attractions such as some popular beaches such as Venice Beach and theme parks such as Six Flags and Universal Studios.

San Francisco

A jumbled collage of colorful neighborhoods and beautiful views, San Francisco draws those free-spirited types who have an eye for edgy art, a taste for imaginative cuisine and a zeal for adventure. It's really not surprising that songwriter Tony Bennett left his heart here: The city boasts jaw-dropping sights, world-class cuisine, cozy cafes and plenty of booming nightlife venues – there's no shortage of ways to stay busy here. Spend an hour or two sunning yourself alongside sea lions on the bay, admiring the views of the city from Twin Peaks, or strolling along the Marina.

Paris

Paris may be very, very, very old (it is atleast more than 2000 years old), but the French metropolis has a young and trendy vibe that attracts an incredible amount of curious people all over the world who must have seen the Eiffel Tower at least once in their life or who must have sat on the steps in front of the Sacré Coeur at least once in their lives.

Barcelona

For days all about Spanish tapas, terraces, sun and beach... Doesn't sound wrong, does it? If you book a city trip to Barcelona, you can combine beach with city! One of the highlights is the Sagrada Familia. And then there's La Rambla: the city's most famous boulevard that you must see at least once. Every Saturday you can also enjoy a free bike tour through Barcelona accompanied by a Dutch guide.

Rome

The famous Colosseum, the Trevi Fountain and the Spanish Steps. This is just a small list of the sights you will encounter during your city trip in Rome. There is so much to see and so much to experience in this Italian city. During your weekend in Rome, don't forget to take regular breaks on one of the terraces on Piazza Navona. With a cappuccino and an Italian gelato of course!
